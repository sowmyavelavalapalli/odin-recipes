Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish made with a flat, round dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as vegetables, meat, or olives. It is baked until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted, making it a favorite food enjoyed by people of all ages.

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Vegetables or meat toppings
  • Oregano or mixed herbs

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Spread tomato sauce on the pizza base.
  3. Add cheese and toppings.
  4. Sprinkle herbs.
  5. Bake until the cheese melts.

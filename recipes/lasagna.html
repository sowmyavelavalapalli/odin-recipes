Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made by layering flat pasta sheets with rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy cheese, and tomato sauce. It is baked until the top becomes golden and the layers are soft and flavorful. Lasagna is often served hot and is loved for its rich taste and filling nature.

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna pasta sheets
  2. Tomato sauce
  3. Cheese
  4. Vegetables or minced meat
  5. Salt and spices

Steps:

  1. Prepare the sauce: Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add minced meat (if using), cook until brown. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer 10–15 minutes.
  2. Cook lasagna sheets if needed: Boil water with salt, cook sheets 6–8 minutes, drain.
  3. Mix the cheese: Combine ricotta/cottage cheese with salt & pepper, optionally add shredded mozzarella.
  4. Assemble: In a baking dish, layer sauce → lasagna sheets → cheese mixture → sauce → mozzarella. Repeat, ending with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil, bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25–30 minutes. Remove foil, bake 10 more minutes to brown cheese.
  6. Serve: Let rest 5–10 minutes, cut into squares, serve hot.
